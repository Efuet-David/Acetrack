Our Story

AceTrack was born from a personal struggle. Our founders, having experienced the overwhelming and often confusing scholarship application process, realized that countless talented students were missing life-changing opportunities simply because they didn't know where to look or how to navigate the complex application landscape.

In 2026, we set out to change that. We built AceTrack to be more than just a scholarship database‚Äîit's a comprehensive platform that guides students through every step of their journey, from discovery to acceptance.

Today, we're proud to have helped over 10,000 students find and win scholarships worth over $50 million in funding. But we're just getting started.
